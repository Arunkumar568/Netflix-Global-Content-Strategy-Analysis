# Netflix Global Content Strategy Analysis

## Executive Summary

This project analyzes Netflix's global content portfolio using Python and business analytics techniques.

The objective is to evaluate content growth, genre strategy, country expansion, audience targeting, and content mix in order to provide strategic recommendations for content investment decisions.

---

# Business Problem

Netflix invests billions of dollars in content production and acquisition.

Business leaders need to understand:

- Which content categories drive growth?
- Which countries contribute the most content?
- What audience segments are being targeted?
- How has Netflix's content strategy evolved over time?

---

# Dataset Information

Dataset: netflix_titles.csv

Total Records: 8,807

Total Columns: 12

Key Fields:

- Type
- Title
- Country
- Release Year
- Rating
- Genre
- Duration

---

# Analysis Performed

## 1. Data Quality Audit

Activities:

- Missing value assessment
- Duplicate record validation
- Data cleaning
- Data standardization

Outcome:

Dataset prepared for business analysis.

---

## 2. Content Growth Analysis

Business Question:

How has Netflix expanded its content portfolio over time?

Key Finding:

Content growth accelerated significantly after 2015.

---

## 3. Genre Strategy Analysis

Business Question:

Which genres dominate Netflix's catalog?

Key Finding:

International Movies and Drama represent the largest content categories.

---

## 4. Country Expansion Analysis

Business Question:

Which countries contribute the most content?

Key Finding:

United States and India are the largest content-producing markets.

---

## 5. Audience Targeting Analysis

Business Question:

Who is Netflix creating content for?

Key Finding:

TV-MA and TV-14 content dominate the platform.

---

## 6. Content Mix Analysis

Business Question:

How is Netflix balancing Movies and TV Shows?

Key Finding:

Movies represent the majority of Netflix content.

---

# Business Recommendations

1. Continue investment in International content.

2. Expand successful Drama and Action categories.

3. Increase content production in high-growth regions.

4. Maintain strong focus on mature audience content.

5. Continue balancing Movies and TV Shows to maximize engagement.
